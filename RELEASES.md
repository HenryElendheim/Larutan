# Releases

## Larutan v0.1.3

Larutan is a dark-mode life-sim about a few small beings living their own lives.
You watch their inner lives -- needs, feelings, memories, goals -- follow any one
of them through a life, and reach in as a god when you want to.

New in this one: the beings grow a culture. They learn skills by doing and get
better at them, form beliefs from what they live, and elders pass both down to the
young -- a conviction can echo down a lineage. They share food with those they care
for, and one scarred by hunger hoards instead. Rarely, someone works out how to tend
the ground so it bears more, and that know-how spreads by being taught. The dead go
further too: a soul can be sent back into a new life, and the long-gone fade to an
epitaph. And whatever the speed, the moments that matter now surface, so you never
miss a birth, a death, or a goal reached.

## Larutan v0.1.2

Larutan is a dark-mode life-sim about a few small beings living their own lives.
You watch their inner lives -- needs, feelings, memories, goals -- follow any one
of them through a life, and reach in as a god when you want to.

New in this one: the dead are their own place now. You can browse them apart from
the living and filter them by realm -- heaven, hell, or purgatory -- follow a soul,
and read its final thought and the thoughts it carried. You can reverse time, too,
and roll the whole world back to an earlier moment. There's a slower "Dwell" speed
for lingering, the map zooms and pans, and the ageless are marked at a glance with
a clear on/off toggle.

## Larutan v0.1.1

Same world, with more to do in it. You can now reach in as a god -- provide food,
warm a being, bless them, inspire a purpose, or make one ageless -- and watch
their own nature take it from there. You can pick who to follow from a roster of
the living and the lost, and the world now saves itself and picks back up exactly
where you left it. Lives also reach for a wider range of goals than before.

## Larutan v0.1

Larutan is a dark-mode life-sim about a few small beings living their own lives.
It simulates their needs, feelings, memories and goals; it lets you follow any
one of them and read their whole inner life; and it runs a grounded world of
seasons where bonds form, generations are born, and lives end and are remembered.
